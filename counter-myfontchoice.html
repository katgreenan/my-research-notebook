This is Volkorn!

This is Open Sans. These two fonts work together because Volkorn is a serif and Open Sans is a sans-serif. This means that they're different enough to stand out from each other! I chose my body font (Open Sans) first, and then decided on a headerfont from there. I wanted something that was readable at a somewhat small size for versatility. But also wanted something that 'felt' me. If I am to use this font in an assessment of who I am, I want to see myself in those words.
